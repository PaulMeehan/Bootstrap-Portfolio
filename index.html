About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio!

I earned my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from North Carolina State University. In my final year of college I began working at Research Triangle Institue (now RTI International) as a computer programmer. Following my time at RTI, I worked at Duke Clinical Research Institute as a programmer and manager.

At Duke, I developed applications to clean and process clinical data submitted from hospitals all over the world. Later, my responsibilities began focusing more on management and less on programming. However, as a manager I missed the satisfaction I enjoyed from writting programs.

Currently, I am taking a bootcamp class on Full Stack Programming to refresh my programming skills and master web application development.
